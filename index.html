Our Services AI to detect suspicious activities related to drug trafficking

Our software leverages AI-driven web scraping to analyze Instagram handles for suspicious activities related to drug trafficking, scanning profiles for specific keywords, unusual behavior, and engagement patterns indicative of substance-related activities.

Technology

Our solution utilizes Python with BeautifulSoup and Selenium for efficient web scraping, leverages TensorFlow or PyTorch for advanced AI/ML pattern recognition, and employs MongoDB to store and manage collected data in JSON format.

Methodology

Our methodology involves scraping Instagram using BeautifulSoup or Selenium for data collection, processing posts, comments, images, and metadata, employing AI-based analysis to detect drug-related keywords and suspicious behavior, and providing alerts and dashboards for flagged profiles.

It efficiently collects a wide range of data, including posts, comments, images, and metadata, to ensure a thorough analysis. Powered by AI, our system identifies drug-related keywords and suspicious behaviors, providing real-time alerts for flagged profiles. The user-friendly dashboards make it easy to visualize insights and track trends, while proxy rotation enhances scraping efficiency and minimizes detection risks.
